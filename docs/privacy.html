Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

GPS Converter is a coordinate conversion utility. This policy explains what data the app does and does not collect.

Data We Collect

None. GPS Converter does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

How the App Works

All coordinate conversions are performed entirely on your device. No coordinates, search history, or usage data are sent anywhere.

Map View

The map feature is powered by Apple Maps (MapKit). When you view a coordinate on the map, Apple's standard MapKit framework is used. Apple's privacy practices apply to any map tile or location data requested by MapKit. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

Third-Party SDKs & Analytics

GPS Converter contains no third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or crash reporting tools.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data.

Children's Privacy

GPS Converter does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised date.
